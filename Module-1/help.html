Community Portal Help Center

Registration Guide

  • Fill in all required fields marked with an asterisk (*)
  • Ensure your phone number follows the format: 123-456-7890
  • Choose your preferred event type from the dropdown
  • Add any special requirements in the message field

Finding Events

  • Use the "Find Nearby Events" button to locate events in your area
  • Browse the image gallery for past event highlights
  • Double-click on any image to enlarge it

Contact Support

If you need additional assistance, please email us at: support@communityportal.com
